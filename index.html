    Thirty Two Lesson Bible Study Course

    About

    This is a thirty-two lesson course on vital issues of concern today. Featuring the major end-time prophecies and other great Bible themes, each lesson points to Scripture passages which answer today's most important questions.

    To begin, you'll need a King James Version Bible which is the textbook for the course. Each lesson contains questions and Bible references. Carefully read each question. Find the text in your Bible, and look for the answer. It might be the entire verse, but usually the question is well answered in a single phrase

    If you have a hard time finding the answer, first check to make sure you have the right verse. Then read the question again. If the verse still does not make sense, go on to the next question. As you continue, the verses that follow should help to clarify it. When you are finished, it is always good to read through the lesson again to get the whole line of connected thought before checking the boxes at the end of the lessons.
