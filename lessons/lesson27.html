    Recognizing God’s Ownership

    1. Who owns the world? Psalm 24:1

    Answer

    The earth is the Lord's

    2. What does God claim as His? Psalm 50:10

    Answer

    Every beast of the forest is mine, and all the cattle upon a thousand hills

    3. What about the world’s wealth? Haggai 2:8

    Answer

    The silver is mine, and the gold is mine, saith the Lord

    4. The kingdom of heaven is as a man travelling into a far country, who did what? Matthew 25:14

    Answer

    Called his own servants, and delivered unto them his goods

    We are “stewards of the mysteries of God.” 1 Corinthians 4:1.

    5. What happens to treasures laid up on earth? Matthew 6:19

    Answer

    Moth and rust doth corrupt, and where thieves break through and steal

    6. What are we to do with the treasures entrusted to us? Matthew 6:20

    Answer

    Lay up for yourselves treasures in heaven

    7. What do our investments show? Matthew 6:21

    Answer

    Where your treasure is, there will your heart be also

    8. If our heart is with the Lord, what question will we ask? Psalm 116:12

    Answer

    What shall I render unto the Lord for all his benefits toward me

    9. What will we bring when we come into His courts? Psalm 96:8

    Answer

    An offering

    10. How much offering shall we give? Deuteronomy 16:17

    Answer

    Every man shall give as he is able, according to the blessing of the Lord

    11. What happens when we give? Luke 6:38

    Answer

    It shall be given unto you; good measure, pressed down, and shaken together, and running over

    12. Wherein have some robbed God? Malachi 3:8

    Answer

    In tithes and offerings

    Notice that tithe and offerings are two different things.

    13. What happens if I rob God? Malachi 3:9

    Answer

    Ye are cursed with a curse

    14. To whom does the tithe belong? Leviticus 27:30

    Answer

    The Lord

    15. What are we to do with what belongs to God? Matthew 22:21

    Answer

    Render...unto God the things that are God's

    16. Ought we to tithe as well as perform the weightier matters of the law? Matthew 23:23

    Answer

    These ought ye to have done, and not to leave the other undone

    17. How should we feel about anything we give to God? 1 Chronicles 29:14 (last half)

    Answer

    All things come of thee, and of thine own have we given thee

    18. What did Abraham give Melchizedek? Genesis 14:20

    Answer

    Tithes of all

    Jesus is “made an high priest for ever after the order of Melchisedec.” Hebrews 6:20. Notice that tithing was practiced by God’s people even before the Levitical priesthood came into existence.

    19. How much is a tithe? Hebrews 7:1, 2

    Answer

    A tenth part of all

    20. How much shall be holy unto the Lord? Leviticus 27:32

    Answer

    The tenth

    21. What did Jacob vow to God? Genesis 28:22

    Answer

    Of all that thou shalt give me, I will surely give the tenth unto thee

    22. Do we return God’s tithe after we have paid everything else, or do we return His tithe first? Proverbs 3:9

    Answer

    Honour the Lord...with the firstfruits of all thine increase

    23. To whom were Israel’s tithes given? Numbers 18:24

    Answer

    To the Levites

    24. What service did they perform which qualified them to receive tithes? Numbers 18:21

    Answer

    The service of the tabernacle

    25. Even so hath the Lord ordained that who should live of the gospel? 1 Corinthians 9:13, 14

    Answer

    They which preach the gospel

    26. Bring ye all the tithes into what? Malachi 3:10

    Answer

    The storehouse

    27. That there may be meat in what? Malachi 3:10

    Answer

    Mine house

    “The house of God...is the church of the living God.” 1 Timothy 3:15.

    28. If we faithfully return His tithe, what will God do?

    Malachi 3:10

    Malachi 3:11

    Answer

    Open you the windows of heaven, and pour you out a blessing, that there shall not be room enough to receive; And I will rebuke the devourer for your sakes, and he shall not destroy the fruit of your ground

    29. What else does He promise? Proverbs 3:10

    Answer

    So shall thy barns be filled with plenty, and thy presses shall burst out with new wine

    30. What is one way of laying up treasure in heaven? Matthew 19:21

    Answer

    Give to the poor

    31. How does that kind of investment bring returns? Proverbs 19:17

    Answer

    He that hath pity upon the poor lendeth unto the Lord; and that which he hath given will he pay him again

    32. What should we remember about God? Deuteronomy 8:18

    Answer

    It is he that giveth thee power to get wealth

    33. If we don’t have as much wealth as we would like, what should we do? Hebrews 13:5

    Answer

    Be content with such things as ye have

    “Better is a little with the fear of the Lord than great treasure and trouble therewith.” Proverbs 15:16.

    “He that loveth silver shall not be satisfied with silver; nor he that loveth abundance with increase: this is also vanity. When goods increase, they are increased that eat them: and what good is there to the owners thereof, saving the beholding of them with their eyes?” Ecclesiastes 5:10, 11.

    “But godliness with contentment is great gain. For we brought nothing into this world, and it is certain we can carry nothing out. And having food and raiment let us be therewith content. But they that will be rich fall into temptation and a snare, and into many foolish and hurtful lusts, which drown men in destruction and perdition. For the love of money is the root of all evil: which while some coveted after, they have erred from the faith, and pierced themselves through with many sorrows. But thou, O man of God, flee these things; and follow after righteousness, godliness, faith, love, patience, meekness.” 1 Timothy 6:6 - 11.

    34. What should we concentrate on? And what is promised? Matthew 6:33

    Answer

    Seek ye first the kingdom of God, and his righteousness; and all these things shall be added unto you
