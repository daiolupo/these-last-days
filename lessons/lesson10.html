    What Is a Soul?

    1. How many souls came out of the loins of Jacob? Exodus 1:5

    Answer

    Seventy souls

    2. How many souls were on Paul’s ship? Acts 27:37

    Answer

    Two hundred threescore and sixteen

    3. What two elements did God use to form a living soul? Genesis 2:7

    Answer

    The dust of the ground, and...the breath of life

    DUST + BREATH = SOUL

    The Biblical definition of a soul is simply a breathing body. Notice that the text does not say that man was given a soul, but rather he became a soul. A soul is not something a person has, it is the person. Souls have blood (Jeremiah 2:34). Not only are people souls, but so are fish and animals (Revelation 16:3).

    The Hebrew word for soul, nephesh, is variously translated “person” (Genesis 14:21), “self” (Leviticus 11:43), “life” (Psalm 31:13), “me” (Judges 16:30), “creature” (Genesis 1:21), “beast” (Leviticus 24:18), “man” (2 Kings 12:4), “thing” (Ezekiel 47:9), and “fish” (Isaiah 19:10). When translated “body” the nephesh is usually dead (Leviticus 21:11).

    The Greek word for soul, psuche, has the same meaning. In Matthew 16:25 Jesus commends anyone who will lose his soul (psuche) for Christ’s sake. It is often translated simply as “life” (Matthew 2:20). It means “person” (Acts 7:14). “My soul” and “your soul” are idiomatic expressions meaning “I” and “you” (Matthew 12:18; 2 Corinthians 12:15, margin).

    4. How is the process God used in creating a soul reversed when we die? Psalm 104:29

    Answer

    Thou takest away their breath, they die, and return to their dust

    SOUL − BREATH = DUST

    “If he set his heart upon man, if he gather unto himself his spirit and his breath; all flesh shall perish together, and man shall turn again unto dust.” Job 34:14, 15.

    5. Can a soul die? Ezekiel 18:20

    Answer

    The soul that sinneth, it shall die

    6. When life ends, what is left? Genesis 3:19

    Answer

    Dust

    7. What returns to God? Ecclesiastes 12:7

    Answer

    The spirit

    8. Where is this spirit located in a person? Job 27:3

    Answer

    In my nostrils

    9. What is it that is found in a person’s nostrils? Genesis 7:22

    Answer

    The breath of life

    The Hebrew word for spirit (and ghost, Job 3:11), ruach, and the Greek word, pneuma, mean breath, wind, or vital element. It is the spark of life which makes you able to live. It is “the breath of the Almighty” (Job 33:4). When the Bible uses those words in reference to man, not once does it say that ruach or pneuma is something in man that retains consciousness after the person dies.

    Angels are called spirits (Psalm 104:4) because to human eyes they are as invisible as breath. But man does not possess the nature of angels (Psalm 8:5), nor does the Bible indicate that he assumes it at death.

    10. What goes forth when man dies? Psalm 146:4

    Answer

    His breath

    11. Is the breath in man any different from the breath in beasts? Ecclesiastes 3:19

    Answer

    They have all one breath

    12. Is there any difference between a dead man and a dead beast? Ecclesiastes 3:20

    Answer

    All go unto one place; all are of the dust, and all turn to dust again

    A soul is like the light that results when a light bulb is connected to a power source. The spirit, or breath of life, is the electric current.

    Electricity will produce light only while it is flowing through the bulb. When the filament in the bulb breaks, the electrical circuit is broken, and the light goes out.

    Just as the light cannot exist unless there is both electricity and a bulb, so there must be both the breath of life and a functional body in order for there to be a living soul.

    13. After a person dies, can he return and appear to those who knew him?

    Job 7:8

    Job 7:9

    Job 7:10

    Job 16:22

    Answer

    The eye of him that hath seen me shall see me no more; He that goeth down to the grave shall come up no more; He shall return no more to his house; I shall go the way whence I shall not return

    14. In these last days some shall depart from the faith, giving heed to what? 1 Timothy 4:1

    Answer

    Seducing spirits

    15. What type of spirits will appear, working miracles? Revelation 16:14

    Answer

    The spirits of devils

    16. In the garden, Satan disguised himself in the form of what? Genesis 3:1

    Answer

    Serpent

    17. Satan can transform himself into a what? 2 Corinthians 11:14

    Answer

    An angel of light

    18. What can his ministers appear as? 2 Corinthians 11:15

    Answer

    Ministers of righteousness

    19. If they speak not according to the law and to the testimony, it is because of what? Isaiah 8:20

    Answer

    Because there is no light in them

    20. Instead of trying to contact the dead, going to mediums and wizards, whom should God’s people seek for truth? Isaiah 8:19

    Answer

    Their God

    21. What types of individuals will not be found among God’s people? Deuteronomy 18:10, 11

    Answer

    That useth divination or an observer of times, or an enchanter, or a witch, or a charmer, or a consulter with familiar spirits, or a wizard

    22. All that do those things are what? Deuteronomy 18:12

    Answer

    An abomination

    23. What words did Paul have for a sorcerer? Acts 13:10

    Answer

    Thou child of the devil, thou enemy of all righteousness

    24. Can a person have fellowship with the Lord and with devils? 1 Corinthians 10:21

    Answer

    Ye cannot drink the cup of the Lord, and the cup of devils: ye cannot be partakers of the Lord's table and...of devils

    25. With Satan’s influences all around us, what does God call us to do? 2 Corinthians 6:17

    Answer

    Come out from among them, and be ye separate, saith the Lord, and touch not the unclean thing

    Christians will have nothing to do with magic, fortune telling, horoscopes or séances; or games, books, movies or toys containing elements of witchcraft or spiritualism.
