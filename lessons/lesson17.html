    How to Give Your Heart to Christ

    1. Why should Jesus save us? Titus 3:5

    Answer

    Not by works of righteousness which we have done, but according to his mercy

    2. How are we saved? Ephesians 2:8, 9

    Answer

    By grace are ye saved through faith; and that not of yourselves: it is the gift of God: not of works

    “Who can say, I have made my heart clean?” “Not by might, nor by power, but by my Spirit, saith the Lord of hosts.” “That your faith should not stand in the wisdom of men, but in the power of God.” Proverbs 20:9; Zechariah 4:6; 1 Corinthians 2:5.

    3. Yet what is an important element in faith? James 2:14 - 26

    Answer

    Works

    “Even a child is known by his doings, whether his work be pure, and whether it be right.” Proverbs 20:11.

    4. Jesus came to call whom to what? Mark 2:17

    Answer

    Sinners to repentance

    5. How does one repent? Isaiah 55:7

    Answer

    Let the wicked forsake his way, and the unrighteousness man his thoughts: and let him return unto the Lord

    6. What should we be asking God to do for us? Psalm 51:2

    Answer

    Wash me throughly from mine iniquity, and cleanse me from my sin

    “Search me, O God, and know my heart: try me, and know my thoughts: and see if there be any wicked way in me, and lead me in the way everlasting.” Psalm 139:23, 24.

    7. When will our search for God be successful? Jeremiah 29:13

    Answer

    Ye shall seek me, and find me, when ye shall search for me with all your heart

    8. What if I regard iniquity in my heart? Psalm 66:18

    Answer

    The Lord will not hear me

    9. Jesus wants people who want to be made what? John 5:6

    Answer

    Whole

    10. Why will only a few receive eternal life? Matthew 7:14

    Answer

    Because strait is the gate, and narrow is the way, which leadeth unto life

    11. Who will not be worthy? Matthew 10:37

    Answer

    He that loveth father or mother more than me...and he that loveth son or daughter more than me

    12. Will God accept a divided commitment? Matthew 6:24

    Answer

    No man can serve two masters

    13. What is the cost of discipleship? Luke 14:33

    Answer

    Whosoever he be of you that forsaketh not all that he hath, he cannot be my disciple

    14. What did Paul say he did daily? 1 Corinthians 15:31

    Answer

    I die daily

    “I am crucified with Christ: nevertheless I live; yet not I, but Christ liveth in me: and the life which I now live in the flesh I live by the faith of the Son of God, who loved me, and gave himself for me.” Galatians 2:20.

    15. If we want Jesus, what do we need to do? Luke 9:23

    Answer

    If any man will come after me, let him deny himself, and take up his cross daily, and follow me

    16. When we come in that way, will He reject us? John 6:37

    Answer

    Him that cometh to me I will in no wise cast out

    17. When we confess our sins to Him, what two things will He do for us? 1 John 1:9

    Answer

    Forgive us our sins, and to cleanse us from all unrighteousness

    18. Must we do anything with our sins besides confessing them? Proverbs 28:13

    Answer

    Confesseth and forsaketh them

    19. When we give up our sins, how far does He remove them from us? Psalm 103:12

    Answer

    As far as the east is from the west

    20. What if we are guilty of the darkest of sins? Isaiah 1:18

    Answer

    Though your sins be as scarlet, they shall be as white as snow

    21. He will do this for us upon what two conditions? Isaiah 1:19

    Answer

    If ye be willing and obedient

    22. Can I obey God without His power? John 15:5

    Answer

    Without me ye can do nothing

    23. Although I do not find it within myself to perform good works, what one thing is present? Romans 7:18

    Answer

    To will

    24. What then is the first step to victory? 2 Corinthians 8:12

    Answer

    If there be first a willing mind

    25. Then what are we to do? And how does it work? 2 Corinthians 8:11

    Answer

    Now therefore perform the doing of it; that as there was a readiness to will, so there may be a performance also out of that which ye have
