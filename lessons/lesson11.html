    The Secret of Immortality

    "In him was life; and the life was the light of men" John 1:4.

    1. What kind of man are we, mortal or immortal? Job 4:17

    Answer

    Mortal

    2. What is your life? James 4:14

    Answer

    A vapor, that appeareth for a little time, and then vanisheth away

    3. What characterizes God the Father and Jesus the Son? John 5:26

    Answer

    As the Father hath life in himself; so hath he given to the Son to have life in himself

    4. Who is eternal and immortal? 1 Timothy 1:17

    Answer

    God

    5. God is the only one who hath what? 1 Timothy 6:16

    Answer

    Immortality

    6. Yet for us Jesus has brought to light the possibility of life and immortality through the what? 2 Timothy 1:10

    Answer

    The gospel

    7. Who only will receive eternal life? John 3:16

    Answer

    Whosoever believeth in him

    8. What word describes the fate of everyone else? John 3:16

    Answer

    Perish

    “He that believeth on the Son hath everlasting life: and he that believeth not the Son shall not see life.” John 3:36.

    9. How does the Bible emphasize that life can exist only when there is a connection to the Lifegiver? 1 John 5:12

    Answer

    He that hath the Son hath life; and he that hath not the Son of God hath not life

    “For in him we live and move, and have our being.” Acts 17:28.

    10. What are the two alternatives? Romans 6:23

    Answer

    The wages of sin is death; but the gift of God is eternal life

    11. At what event will this mortal put on immortality? 1 Corinthians 15:52, 53

    Answer

    At the last trump

    12. When will the unfading crown be received? 1 Peter 5:4

    Answer

    When the chief Shepherd shall appear

    “When Christ, who is our life, shall appear, then shall ye also appear with him in glory.” Colossians 3:4.

    13. What two trees grew in the midst of Eden? Genesis 2:9

    Answer

    The tree of life...and the tree of knowledge of good and evil

    14. What was the only tree in the garden from which Adam and Eve were forbidden to eat? Genesis 2:16, 17

    Answer

    The tree of knowledge of good and evil

    15. What punishment did God decree for disobedience? Genesis 2:17

    Answer

    Thou shalt surely die

    16. What did Satan say? Genesis 3:4

    Answer

    Ye shall not surely die

    17. What are we told about the devil, the truth and lies? John 8:44

    Answer

    There is no truth in him...he is a liar, and the father of it

    18. Once he sinned, from what tree would Adam have had to eat in order to live forever? Genesis 3:22

    Answer

    The tree of life

    19. Because He does not permit sinners to live forever, what did God do? Genesis 3:24

    Answer

    He drove out the man; and he placed at the east of the garden of Eden cherubims, and a flaming sword

    20. What river will be in the new earth? Revelation 22:1

    Answer

    River of water of life

    21. What will grow on either side of the river? Revelation 22:2

    Answer

    The tree of life

    22. Who only will have right to the tree of life? Revelation 22:14

    Answer

    They that do his commandments

    23. But don’t sinners also have some undying spark of life in them? 1 John 3:15

    Answer

    No murderer hath eternal life abiding in him

    “The soul that sinneth, it shall die.” Ezekiel 18:20.

    24. If we want life, what must we do? Matthew 19:17

    Answer

    Keep the commandments

    25. By what means do we seek for immortality and eternal life? Romans 2:7

    Answer

    Patient continuance in well doing

    26.What will happen to all evildoers? Psalm 37:9

    Answer

    Shall be cut off

    27. Will the wicked be any more? Psalm 37:10

    Answer

    The wicked shall not be; yea thou shalt diligently consider his place, and it shall not be

    28. Into what will they consume away? Psalm 37:20

    Answer

    Smoke

    In the Light of God's Word...
