    A Day to Remember

    1. What and what were the first day? Genesis 1:5

    Answer

    The evening and the morning

    2. What and what were the second day? Genesis 1:8

    Answer

    The evening and the morning

    3. From when unto when shall ye celebrate your Sabbath? Leviticus 23:32

    Answer

    From even unto even

    4. “Even” meant when the sun did what? Mark 1:32

    Answer

    Set

    5. At what time of day were the gates of Jerusalem shut for the Sabbath? Nehemiah 13:19

    Answer

    When the gates of Jerusalem began to be dark before the sabbath

    6. It is lawful to do what on the Sabbath days? Matthew 12:12

    Answer

    To do well

    7. God tells me that I am to turn away from doing what on His holy day? Isaiah 58:13

    Answer

    Thy pleasure

    8. The Sabbath is called what three things? Isaiah 58:13

    Answer

    A delight, the holy of the Lord, honourable

    9. I shall honor Him not doing what three things on that day? Isaiah 58:13

    Answer

    Not doing thine own ways, nor finding thine own pleasure, nor speaking thine own words

    10. Then what promise is given? Isaiah 58:14

    Answer

    Then shalt thou delight thyself in the Lord

    11. What are the first six days of the week called? Ezekiel 46:1

    Answer

    The six working days

    12. What are we not to do on the Sabbath? Exodus 20:10

    Answer

    In it thou shalt not do any work

    The rest of the verse indicates that we are not to employ others to do secular work for us on the Sabbath either.

    13. What about in harvest time or in other times when there is much urgent work to be done? Exodus 34:21

    Answer

    In earing time and in harvest thou shalt rest

    14. What instructions illustrate how certain household details are not to be taken care of during the Sabbath hours? Exodus 35:3

    Answer

    Ye shall kindle no fire throughout your habitations upon the sabbath day

    15. What is Friday called which indicates that all cooking and cleaning for the Sabbath is to be done the day before? Luke 23:54; Mark 15:42

    Answer

    The preparation

    16. What did Nehemiah say to those who purchased food and wares from merchants on the Sabbath? Nehemiah 13:16, 17

    Answer

    What evil thing is this that ye do, and profane the sabbath day?

    17. What are we not to forsake? Hebrews 10:25

    Answer

    The assembling of ourselves together

    18.The Sabbath of rest is for an holy what? Leviticus 23:3

    Answer

    Convocation

    The Hebrew word used here means, “A public meeting...assembly, calling, reading.” (Strong’s Concordance)

    19. What did Jesus say about where there are only two or three gathered together in His name? Matthew 18:20

    Answer

    There am I in the midst of them

    20. The ruler of the synagogue was indignant because Jesus had done what on the Sabbath day? Luke 13:14

    Answer

    Healed

    21. In reply, Jesus declared that those whom Satan has bound may be what on the Sabbath? Luke 13:16

    Answer

    Loosed from this bond

    In following our Lord’s example, we may find many ways on the Sabbath day to loose others from Satan’s bonds, bringing encouragement to the lonely, and hope to those in need of comfort and cheer. Some suggestions may be found in Matthew 25:35-40.

    22. The Sabbath is to remind us of what? Genesis 2:1, 2

    Answer

    On the seventh day God ended his work

    Through nature, we may be brought closer to our Creator on the Sabbath day. As a weekly memorial of God’s perfect creation, the Sabbath hours may be appropriately spent in a contemplation of His handiwork, as well as in communion with Him through Bible study and prayer.

    23. What has the Lord said about those who can’t wait for the Sabbath to be over? Amos 8:5, 7

    Answer

    Surely I will never forget any of their works

    24. What will we do in the Lord’s day? Psalm 118:24

    Answer

    We will rejoice and be glad in it

    25. God’s blessing is upon those who do what? Isaiah 56:2

    Answer

    That keepeth the sabbath from polluting it

    26. In the earth made new, how often will all flesh come to worship before the Lord? Isaiah 66:22, 23

    Answer

    From one new moon to another, and from one sabbath to another
