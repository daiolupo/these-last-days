    Invisible Forces Behind the Scenes

    1. Did God create only visible things? Colossians 1:16

    Answer

    Colossian 1:16 For by him were all things created, that are in heaven, and that are in earth, visible and invisible, whether they be thrones, or dominions, or principalities, or powers: all things were created by him, and for him.

    Visible and invisible

    2. What superhuman beings does the Bible mention? Psalm 8.5

    Answer

    Psalm 8:5 For thou hast made him a little lower than the angels, and hast crowned him with glory and honour.

    Angels

    3. Has anyone ever seen an angel? Numbers 22:31

    Answer

    Numbers 22:31 Then the Lord opened the eyes of Balaam, and he saw the angel of the Lord standing in the way, and his sword drawn in his hand: and he bowed down his head, and fell flat on his face.

    The Lord opened the eyes of Balaam and he saw the angel of the Lord

    4. What type of angels were stationed at the entrance to the garden of Eden when man was driven out? Genesis 3:24

    Answer

    Genesis 3:24 So he drove out the man; and he placed at the east of the garden of Eden Cherubims, and a flaming sword which turned every way, to keep the way of the tree of life.

    Cherubims

    Angels are not the spirits of dead people as some may suppose. No one had yet died when Adam and Eve were driven from Eden. The Bible speaks definitely of the existence of intelligent beings in the universe before the earth was created (Job 38:4, 7).

    5. Are angels very strong? Psalm 103:20

    Answer

    Psalm 103:20 Bless the Lord, ye his angels, that excel in strength, that do his commandments, hearkening unto the voice of his word.

    Excel in strength

    6. How swiftly do they move? Ezekiel 1:14

    Answer

    Ezekiel 1:14 And the living creatures ran and returned as the appearance of a flash of lightning.

    As the appearance of a flash of lightning

    7. What have they been sent forth to do? Hebrews 1:14

    Answer

    Hebrews 1:14 Are they not all ministering spirits, sent forth to minister for them who shall be heirs of salvation?

    To minister for them who shall be heirs of salvation

    8. How might we meet an angel some day? Hebrews 13:2

    Answer

    Hebrews 13:2 Be not forgetful to entertain strangers: for thereby some have entertained angels unawares.

    Be not forgetful to entertain strangers: for thereby some have entertained angels unawares

    9. How do angels protect God’s people from danger? Psalm 34:7

    Answer

    The angel of the Lord encampeth round about them that fear him, and delivereth them

    Here are a few stories of angels helping God’s people in times of personal crisis: 1 Kings 19:1 - 8; Daniel 6:16 - 23; Acts 5:17 - 23; Acts 12:5 - 11.

    10. How will God protect His people through the calamities of the last days? Psalm 91:11

    Answer

    He shall give his angels charge over thee, to keep thee in all thy ways

    “Be not afraid of sudden fear, neither of the desolation of the wicked, when it cometh. For the Lord shall be thy confidence, and shall keep thy foot from being taken.” Proverbs 3:25, 26.

    11. Are angels assigned even to children? Matthew 18:10

    Answer

    Matthew 18:10 Take heed that ye despise not one of these little ones; for I say unto you, That in heaven their angels do always behold the face of my Father which is in heaven.

    Their angels do always behold the face of my Father

    12. How does the Bible describe the loyalty of the angels to God? Psalm 103:20

    Answer

    Psalm 103:20 Bless the Lord, ye his angels, that excel in strength, that do his commandments, hearkening unto the voice of his word.

    That do his commandments, hearkening unto the voice of his word

    13. God spared not the angels that did what? 2 Peter 2:4

    Answer

    2 Peter 2:4 For if God spared not the angels that sinned, but cast them down to hell, and delivered them into chains of darkness, to be reserved unto judgment

    Sinned

    14. What is sin? 1 John 3:4

    Answer

    1 John 3:4 Whosoever committeth sin transgresseth also the law: for sin is the transgression of the law.

    The transgression of the law

    15. Who is the leader of the rebellious angels? Revelation 12:9

    Answer

    Revelation 12:9 And the great dragon was cast out, that old serpent, called the Devil, and Satan, which deceiveth the whole world: he was cast out into the earth, and his angels were cast out with him.

    The Devil, and Satan

    16. What was he called before he fell? Isaiah 14:12

    Answer

    Isaiah 14:12 How art thou fallen from heaven, O Lucifer, son of the morning! how art thou cut down to the ground, which didst weaken the nations!

    Lucifer

    17. What position did he hold in heaven? Ezekiel 28:14

    Answer

    Ezekiel 28:14 Thou art the anointed cherub that covereth; and I have set thee so: thou wast upon the holy mountain of God; thou hast walked up and down in the midst of the stones of fire.

    The anointed cherub that covereth

    18. What was he like from the day he was created until iniquity was found in him? Ezekiel 28:15

    Answer

    Ezekiel 28:15 Thou wast perfect in thy ways from the day that thou wast created, till iniquity was found in thee.

    Perfect

    19. In three words or less, what did Lucifer do that God cast him out and took away his position as covering cherub? Ezekiel 28:16

    Answer

    Ezekiel 28:16 By the multitude of thy merchandise they have filled the midst of thee with violence, and thou hast sinned: therefore I will cast thee as profane out of the mountain of God: and I will destroy thee, O covering cherub, from the midst of the stones of fire.

    Thou hast sinned

    20. What sin did he first commit in his heart? Ezekiel 28:17

    Answer

    Ezekiel 28:17 Thine heart was lifted up because of thy beauty, thou hast corrupted thy wisdom by reason of thy brightness: I will cast thee to the ground, I will lay thee before kings, that they may behold thee.

    Thine heart was lifted up because of thy beauty, thou hast corrupted thy wisdom by reason of thy brightness

    21. Whose position did he covet? Isaiah 14:13, 14

    Answer

    Isaiah 14:13 For thou hast said in thine heart, I will ascend into heaven, I will exalt my throne above the stars of God: I will sit also upon the mount of the congregation, in the sides of the north: 14 I will ascend above the heights of the clouds; I will be like the most High.

    The most High

    “Every one that exalteth himself shall be abased; and he that humbleth himself shall be exalted.” Luke 18:14.

    22. This led to what in heaven? Revelation 12:7

    Answer

    Revelation 12:7 And there was war in heaven: Michael and his angels fought against the dragon; and the dragon fought and his angels,

    War

    23. What percentage of the angels (symbolically referred to as “stars”) joined Satan in his rebellion? Revelation 12:4

    Answer

    Revelation 12:4 And his tail drew the third part of the stars of heaven, and did cast them to the earth: and the dragon stood before the woman which was ready to be delivered, for to devour her child as soon as it was born.

    The third part

    24. Did their rebellion succeed? Revelation 12:8

    Answer

    And prevailed not; neither was their place found any more in heaven

    25. What happened to Satan and his angels? Revelation 12:9

    Answer

    Revelation 12:9 And the great dragon was cast out, that old serpent, called the Devil, and Satan, which deceiveth the whole world: he was cast out into the earth, and his angels were cast out with him.

    Cast out into the earth, and his angels were cast out with him

    26. They are now being reserved in what condition until the judgment of the great day? Jude 6

    Answer

    Jude 6 And the angels which kept not their first estate, but left their own habitation, he hath reserved in everlasting chains under darkness unto the judgment of the great day.

    In everlasting chains under darkness

    The Bible speaks of the fallen angels as “unclean spirits,” “evil spirits,” and “devils.” Their work of darkness is limited to this world. For an illustration of their destructive work upon man and beast when unrestrained by the mighty angels of God, read Mark 5:1 - 13.

    27. What is Satan doing now? 1 Peter 5:8

    Answer

    1 Peter 5:8 Be sober, be vigilant; because your adversary the devil, as a roaring lion, walketh about, seeking whom he may devour:

    As a roaring lion, walketh about, seeking whom he may devour

    28. Why has he come to us now in great wrath? Revelation 12:12

    Answer

    Revelation 12:12 Therefore rejoice, ye heavens, and ye that dwell in them. Woe to the inhabiters of the earth and of the sea! for the devil is come down unto you, having great wrath, because he knoweth that he hath but a short time.

    Because he knoweth that he hath but a short time

    29. What shall we do to stand against him? Ephesians 6:11

    Answer

    Ephesians 6:11 Put on the whole armour of God, that ye may be able to stand against the wiles of the devil.

    Put on the whole armour of God

    “For we wrestle not against flesh and blood, but against principalities, against powers, against the rulers of the darkness of this world, against spiritual wickedness in high places.” Ephesians 6:12.

    30. What armor do we need?

    Ephesians 6:14

    Loins girded with

    Breastplate of

    (verse 15) Feet shod with

    (verse 16) Shield of

    (verse 17) Helmet of

    Sword of

    which is

    Answer

    Ephesians 6:14-17 - 14Stand therefore, having your loins girt about with truth, and having on the breastplate of righteousness; 15 And your feet shod with the preparation of the gospel of peace; 16 Above all, taking the shield of faith, wherewith ye shall be able to quench all the fiery darts of the wicked. 17 And take the helmet of salvation, and the sword of the Spirit, which is the word of God:

    Loins girded with: Truth

    Breastplate of: Righteousness

    (verse 15) Feet shod with: The gospel of peace

    (verse 16) Shield of: Faith

    (verse 17) Helmet of: Salvation

    Sword of: The Spirit

    which is: The word of God

    31. In what three traps does Satan get people? 1 John 2:16

    Answer

    1 John 2:16 For all that is in the world, the lust of the flesh, and the lust of the eyes, and the pride of life, is not of the Father, but is of the world.

    The lust of the flesh, and the lust of the eyes, and the pride of life

    Although Satan makes his offers appear enticing, his purpose is to destroy people. Job 1:6 - 22 unveils the real conflict going on behind the scenes over each one of us.

    32. What specific action must we take? James 4:7, 8

    Answer

    James 4:7, 8 - Submit yourselves therefore to God. Resist the devil, and he will flee from you. 8 Draw nigh to God, and he will draw nigh to you. Cleanse your hands, ye sinners; and purify your hearts, ye double minded.

    Submit yourselves therefore unto God. Resist the devil, and he will flee from you. Draw nigh to God

    33. If we stay close to God, what promise may we claim when Satan bombards us? Isaiah 59:19 (last half)

    Answer

    Isaiah 59:19 So shall they fear the name of the Lord from the west, and his glory from the rising of the sun. When the enemy shall come in like a flood, the Spirit of the Lord shall lift up a standard against him.

    When the enemy shall come in like a flood, the Spirit of the Lord shall lift up a standard against him

    34. How are the angels of God affected when we decide to place ourselves on God’s side? Luke 15:10

    Answer

    Luke 15:10 Likewise, I say unto you, there is joy in the presence of the angels of God over one sinner that repenteth.

    There is joy in the presence of the angels of God over one sinner that repenteth
